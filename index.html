About

MindSpace AI is an early-stage mental wellness platform that uses AI to monitor user sentiment and behavioral patterns, helping individuals and organizations detect burnout and emotional stress early. We combine sentiment analysis, journaling prompts, and predictive analytics to support mental clarity before crisis hits.

Why MindSpace?

  • AI-powered burnout and mood detection
  • Personalized well-being recommendations
  • Designed for students, employees, and remote teams
  • Privacy-first and user-centric design

Our Vision

To empower individuals to understand and take control of their emotional well-being through early intervention, data-driven support, and compassion-centered design.
